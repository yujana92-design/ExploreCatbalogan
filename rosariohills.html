Rosario Hills Resort

Rosario Hills is in Brgy. New Mahayag, Catbalogan City — perched up high so you get those lush hillside + mountain/valley vibes whenever you stay. It’s built for people who want to get away from city stress: comfy rooms with a rustic‑but‑mountain‑house feel, wide open space, and a vibe that leans more “relax & reconnect with nature” rather than party‑resort.

They’ve got a legit swimming pool (adult + kiddie friendly), which is super nice if you just want to chill or cool off after a hike. Also good for fams or groups: there’s a playground with swings, slides, monkey‑bars and all — nice if you bring kids (or just want to feel like a kid again).

You get fresh mountain air, peaceful surroundings, and enough comforts (rooms, nice pool, open space) to chill well. If you want a break from the chaos and just want good views + good rest — this resort nails it.
