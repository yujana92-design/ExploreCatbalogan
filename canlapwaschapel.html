Canlapwas Chapel

Canlapwas Chapel, officially known as Our Lady of Salvacion Church, is a local Catholic chapel situated along Canlapwas Road in Barangay Canlapwas, Catbalogan City, Samar, Philippines. It’s listed among the key Catholic worship sites in the area, serving the spiritual needs of residents in this busy barangay of Catbalogan. The chapel operates as part of the city’s broader network of Catholic churches, where faithful gather for Mass and community religious events.

Barangay Canlapwas itself is one of the many barangays that make up Catbalogan City — a province capital with deep historical roots first established by Spanish missionaries in the late 1500s. While the chapel doesn’t have extensive online historical records, it’s clear it functions as an active religious visita or chapel community hub within the predominantly Catholic culture of the city. The devotion carried within its walls reflects the widespread Marian and Catholic traditions in the Philippines.

Our Lady of Salvacion Church plays a daily role beyond prayer — it’s part of the social fabric in Canlapwas, a barangay that recently saw youth and community initiatives like health and awareness programs tied to local development efforts. Although specifics about the chapel’s founding date or architectural style aren’t widely published online, its presence is a spiritual anchor in the neighborhood and a part of Catbalogan’s rich tapestry of church landmarks.
