Toril Grill and Resto Bar

Toril Grill & Resto Bar sits in Brgy. Socorro, Catbalogan City. It offers a mix of local Filipino dishes plus some continental and international‑style fare. People mention soups (like lomi), pizzas (even Hawaiian‑style), grilled meats, seafood, and rice meals among their go‑to orders.

They’re open daily, roughly from 10:00 AM to midnight. The place has outdoor seating and offers a chill, resto‑bar vibe — good for lunch, dinner, or nighttime hangouts. The staff seems friendly and attentive: one review singled out the “very attentive and friendly” crew plus a “delicious lomi.”

Toril Grill & Resto Bar gets props for its view — apparently you can catch a nice overlook of Maqueda Bay (or at least scenic surroundings), especially if you go at the right time. For groups or families, it’s convenient: they offer a variety of dishes (from budget‑friendly to heftier meals), and there seems to be enough parking, which helps when you roll in by car or habal‑habal.
