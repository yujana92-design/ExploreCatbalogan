Perseveranda Catbalogan

Perseveranda Memorial Park is a private memorial/cemetery park in Catbalogan City, Samar. It’s located in or near Barangay Mercedes (accessible via Diversion Road), and is just a few kilometers away from Catbalogan’s city proper — so fairly easy to get to.

It’s described as a “serene and dignified memorial park” that offers lawn lots, mausoleum spaces, and managed cemetery plots for families to honor loved ones. According to one listing, the memorial lots are marketed with monthly amortization plans (e.g. modest monthly payments) — making it a (relatively) affordable option for long‑term burial planning. The park claims to have proper documentation (clear title, occupancy permit) and is zoned as non‑residential — meant for memorial use.

Perseveranda offers an alternative to crowded public cemeteries in the area, giving families peace of mind with a planned, organized resting place for their loved ones. Because of its proximity to the city proper but still more controlled/managed than older cemeteries, it may appeal to people who want accessibility + order. Also, as a private memorial park, it might give families more flexibility when it comes to lot maintenance, security, and long‑term upkeep (compared to older, public‑run cemeteries).
