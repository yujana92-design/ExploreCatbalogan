Tina's Garden

Tina's Garden is a beloved local eatery nestled along San Francisco Street in the heart of Catbalogan City — known to many as a go-to spot for cooling down, quick bites, and easy chats with friends. It’s especially famous for its signature halo-halo, which locals swear by: layered generously with fruits (including a standout pineapple twist), shaved ice, and milk — perfect for beating the Samar heat.

But Tina’s Garden isn’t just about a sweet treat. It offers a selection of traditional snacks and comfort foods that tug at nostalgia: think golden-brown banana fritters, chewy cassava cake, and soft rice cakes (like “Puto Kalinayan”) — all made with local ingredients that reflect Samar’s culinary soil. The vibe is simple, homey, and unpretentious — a place where locals drop by mid-afternoon for a refreshing dessert or a quick meal, and where travelers can get a genuine taste of Catbalogan everyday life.

Tina’s Garden stands out as a slice of community life — a chill corner where flavors, culture, and small-town warmth meet. If you’re strolling Catbalogan’s city proper and craving a local flavor or a spot to slow down, this place gives that cozy, unbranded charm that feels like home.
