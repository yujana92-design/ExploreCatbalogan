Bangon Falls

Bangon Falls is described as a multilevel waterfall tucked in the hinterlands — located in Barangay San Andres, Catbalogan, Samar. The falls features natural pools and layered cascades — ideal for swimming, canyoning or just relaxing in the water under lush tropical greenery.

For the adventurous: reaching Bangon Falls involves a bit of work. There’s reportedly around a one‑hour trek from the jump‑off point to the falls. Visitors say some parts have steep, slippery stones, so proper footwear and care are a must — but once you get there, you’re greeted by cool deep pools and dramatic cascades.

Because of its potential for adventure (swimming, canyoning, hiking) and its natural beauty, Bangon Falls is considered one of the gems of Samar’s eco‑tourism offerings.
