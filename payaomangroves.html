Payao Mangroves Resort

Payao Mangroves Resort is a serene eco-tourism destination in Catbalogan City, surrounded by lush mangrove forests and calm coastal waters that showcase the area’s rich natural beauty. The resort is known for its peaceful atmosphere, making it a perfect spot for nature lovers, families, and visitors seeking a quiet retreat away from the busy city. Boardwalks and viewing areas allow guests to explore the mangroves while enjoying fresh sea air and scenic views.

The resort promotes environmental awareness by highlighting the importance of mangrove conservation, which helps protect coastlines and support marine life. Visitors can enjoy leisurely walks, simple seaside dining, and relaxing moments amidst the greenery, often accompanied by the soothing sounds of birds and waves. It is also a favorite for casual gatherings, picnics, and educational trips focused on nature and sustainability.

Payao Mangroves Resort serves as a living reminder of Catbalogan’s commitment to preserving its natural treasures. Whether for sightseeing, learning about mangrove ecosystems, or simply unwinding in a quiet coastal setting, the resort offers a refreshing experience that connects visitors with both nature and local heritage.
