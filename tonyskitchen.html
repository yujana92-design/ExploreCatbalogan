Tony's Kitchen

Tony’s Kitchen is a beloved local eatery located on San Roque Street in the heart of Catbalogan City, known for serving hearty, home-style meals that attract both locals and visitors.

The menu focuses on Filipino comfort food with generous portions, featuring favorites like braised pork leg (Pata Tim), lumpia, and savory pancit dishes that keep diners coming back for more. The flavors are rich, nostalgic, and crafted to satisfy both casual meals and celebratory gatherings.

Tony’s Kitchen is also a cozy hub in Catbalogan where friends and families gather, enjoy delicious food, and create warm memories in an inviting, unpretentious setting.
