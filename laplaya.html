La Playa De Bohol

La Playa De Bohol is a beach resort in Catbalogan (Barangay Pangdan / Sitio Madalunot area), offering a laid‑back coastal escape for locals and travelers alike. The resort provides cottages, tents, and cabins, giving guests flexible options whether you’re there for a day‑use dip or an overnight stay.

During the day, you can chill by the shore, enjoy swimming, or just lounge in the beachside cottages — it’s ideal for a quick getaway from city life. For overnight stays, La Playa De Bohol keeps things simple but cozy: expect a laid‑back resort vibe, accessible pricing (with entrance fee + cottage/tent or cabin rental), and the calming sounds of the sea to lull you into rest.

Whether you’re planning a chill day‑trip with friends or a low-key overnight escape, La Playa De Bohol offers a relaxed seaside spot to unwind and enjoy Catbalogan’s coastal charm.
